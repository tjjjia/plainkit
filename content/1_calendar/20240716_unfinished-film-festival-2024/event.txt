Description:

Are you haunted by a film you never made, an unfinished film, or a film you thought was finished but now want to rework again? Participate in the 2024 Unfinished Film Festival and camp with us at Odapark!

To apply, send us a link to one of your films, and a short video (max 3min.) about your relationship to your unfinished film and/or interesting challenges in your life as a filmmaker. Participation is free, including meals and a tent.

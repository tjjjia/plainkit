Description:

The KABK Graduation Show 2024 is on view from Thursday 27 June to Tuesday 2 July. Discover the work of the art and design graduates, presented in an 8,000 m2 exhibition space in the historic academy building.

Go on a journey of discovery through the corridors and be inspired by performances, illustrations, graphic design, installations, photography, film, painting and sound art.

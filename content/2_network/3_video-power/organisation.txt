Description:

Video Power is an audiovisual platform based in Maastricht. 
We focus on filmmakers whose drive is deeply personal and who shape the meaning of their films organically. 
As a creative sparring partner, we specialize in gearing production and distribution to the specific nature of each project. 
Since 2013, Video Power has produced films that put across a distinct view of the world and are recognized on leading national and international stages.
We use our experience to support and unite adventurous creators.
